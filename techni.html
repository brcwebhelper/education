Technical Questions

What are Technical Questions?

Technical questions assess the specific knowledge and skills required for a particular job or field. They often focus on problem-solving, understanding of concepts, and practical application of knowledge.

Why are Technical Questions Important?

Technical questions are crucial in evaluating a candidate's ability to perform job-specific tasks. They help employers determine if the candidate possesses the necessary technical skills and knowledge to be successful in the role.

Benefits of Technical Questions

  • Identify technical proficiency and problem-solving skills.
  • Ensure candidates have relevant and up-to-date knowledge.
  • Differentiate between candidates with similar qualifications.
  • Assess practical application of theoretical concepts.

Sample Technical Question

What is the time complexity of bubble sort in the worst case?
